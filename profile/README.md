## Overview

Sound Control Advanced is an advanced per-application audio control utility that gives you independent volume, equalizer, balance, and output device assignment for every application running on your system, providing the audio management granularity that macOS's single system volume slider cannot offer. It solves the common frustration of one application being too loud while another is too quiet, or needing music to play through headphones while notifications come through speakers — scenarios that require per-application audio control.

Per-application volume controls appear in a unified panel showing every application that is producing audio, each with its own independent volume slider. Adjusting one application's volume does not affect any other application, enabling precise audio balance across simultaneous sources. The volume controls persist between application launches, maintaining your preferred levels without readjustment.

Per-application EQ applies equalization to individual applications, enabling audio optimization tailored to each source — boosting bass for a music player, enhancing voice clarity for a video call application, reducing harshness in a podcast player. The EQ is a multi-band parametric equalizer with preset and custom curve options that provide professional-grade tone shaping.

Per-application output routing sends each application's audio to a different output device — music to Bluetooth headphones, video calls to the built-in speakers, notifications to a desk speaker — without changing the system default output. This capability handles the multi-device audio scenarios that macOS's single-output model cannot address.

For anyone who runs multiple audio-producing applications simultaneously and wants independent control over each one's volume, tone, and output destination, Sound Control Advanced is the audio management tool that provides the per-application control that macOS should include but does not.
